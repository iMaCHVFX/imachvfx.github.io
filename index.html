The Goals

Variety

I believe that the more variety you have access to when learning, the more motivation you have to learn.

Authenticity

Only authentic texts from the time these languages were spoken, written, and read. Allowing you to learn with context with very little hassle.

Have Fun

I wanted a tool that was academically sound, but also was fun, and kept the dopamine flowing. So I took the scoring of one of my favorite Video Games and applied it to ancient greek. expanding to other languages
