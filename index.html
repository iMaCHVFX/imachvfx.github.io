Features

Contextual Vocabulary Mining

As you type, instant definitions appear for words. The system automatically generates Anki flashcards that preserve the sentence context (N+1 learning), ensuring you learn vocabulary as it functions in real literature, not just isolated lists.

Immediate Feedback Loop

The "gamified" scoring system (300/100/50/Miss & Combo) provides instant validation of your accuracy. This dopamine-driven feedback loop keeps you focused for longer sessions, turning what is usually a grueling study hour into a high-engagement "play" session

Active Recall Engine

Unlike passive reading, MnemoType forces you to type every diacritic (breathing marks, accents, iota subscripts). This mechanically encodes the correct spelling and pronunciation into your muscle memory.
